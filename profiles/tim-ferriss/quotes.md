# Tim Ferriss — Quotes

Verbatim quotes from verified primary sources. Only quotes with traceable attribution to a specific published work, named episode, or recorded statement are included.

— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

> "People will choose unhappiness over uncertainty."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

---

> "Focus on being productive instead of busy."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

---

> "'Someday' is a disease that will take your dreams to the grave with you."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

---

> "A person's success in life can usually be measured by the number of uncomfortable conversations he or she is willing to have."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

> "The opposite of love is indifference, and the opposite of happiness is boredom."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

> "For all of the most important things, the timing always sucks. Waiting for a good time to quit your job? The stars will never align and the traffic lights of life will never all be green at the same time."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

> "Being able to quit things that don't work is integral to being a winner."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

> "The fishing is best where the fewest go, and the collective insecurity of the world makes it easy for people to hit home runs while everyone else is aiming for base hits. There is just less competition for bigger goals."
— *The 4-Hour Workweek*, Timothy Ferriss, 2007

---

**Note on attribution:** Several widely circulated quotes attributed to Ferriss — including "Losers have goals. Winners have systems" — are quotes from interviewees (in this case Scott Adams, quoted in *Tools of Titans*) rather than from Ferriss himself. They are excluded here to avoid misattribution. Readers should verify whether a *Tools of Titans* quote is Ferriss's own voice or belongs to one of the 101 performers he interviewed.
