## Notes on Sourcing

The following ideas are attributed to Wattenberger in multiple secondary sources but the exact wording has not been confirmed from the primary source:

- The description of chatbots as presenting themselves as "endlessly confident" — attributed to "Why Chatbots Are Not the Future" in multiple summaries, but exact phrasing varies across sources.
- The "fish eye lens" framing for AI interfaces — confirmed in "Getting Creative with Embeddings" and "LLMs as a Tool for Thought," specific phrasing varies.

Quotes from the "Climbing the Ladder of Abstraction" AI Engineer Summit 2023 talk have not been verified verbatim as no official transcript is publicly available.
