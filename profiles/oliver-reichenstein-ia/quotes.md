# Oliver Reichenstein — Verified Quotes

The following quotes are drawn from iA essays, interviews, and talks. Each is cited with source and year. Only quotes with verifiable provenance are included; attribution is not inferred.

---

> "95% of the information on the web is written language. It is only logical to say that a web designer should get good training in the main discipline of shaping written information, that is: typography."

— "Web Design is 95% Typography," ia.net, October 2006

---

> "Web design is not about picking great typefaces, it is how we use them."

— "Web Design is 95% Typography," ia.net, October 2006

---

> "Typography in practice is not choosing fonts or making fonts, it's about shaping text for optimal user experience."

— "Reactions to 95% Typography," ia.net, November 2006

---

> "Focus on typography, ask yourself, which text is functional, which text is passive, systematize them, order them hierarchically, simplify, create an interface."

— "Reactions to 95% Typography," ia.net, November 2006

---

> "Good design is invisible. Good screen design happens in the subatomic level of microtypography, the invisible grid of macrotypography, and the invisible world of interaction design."

— "Good Design is Invisible," ia.net / The Verge interview, July 2012

---

> "The biggest motivation to build iA Writer came from the mad idea to create our own hardware. A digital writing machine."

— "Good Design is Invisible," ia.net / The Verge interview, July 2012

---

> "We plan to continue both working for clients and on products, because we feel that both benefit from the experience we gain in each field."

— "Good Design is Invisible," ia.net / The Verge interview, July 2012
