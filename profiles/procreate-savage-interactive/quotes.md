# Procreate & Savage Interactive — Quotes

Verified quotes from James Cuda (CEO and co-founder) and Claire d'Este (Chief Product Officer), sourced from named interviews, published statements, and press coverage. No quotes have been fabricated or paraphrased without attribution.

---

## James Cuda

> "I really f— hate generative AI. I don't like what's happening in the industry and I don't like what it's doing to artists. We're not going to be introducing any generative AI into our products."
— James Cuda, Procreate social media post and press coverage, August 2024 (Fortune, TechCrunch, ProVideo Coalition)

> "We're never going there. Creativity is made, not generated."
— James Cuda, Procreate official statement, August 2024

> "Generative AI is ripping the humanity out of things. Built on a foundation of theft, the technology is steering us toward a barren future."
— Savage Interactive official statement (attributed to company under Cuda's leadership), August 2024

> "We are not going to be introducing any generative AI into our products. Our products are always designed and developed with the idea that a human will be creating something."
— James Cuda, August 2024 (multiple outlets including Fortune and Fast Company)

> "We don't know exactly where this story's gonna go or how it ends, but we believe that we're on the right path supporting human creativity."
— James Cuda, August 2024

> "We've never taken VC funding or succumbed to acquisition offers. We're here developing Procreate thanks to each and every sale from our community."
— James Cuda, The Design Est interview

> "It's a human condition to want to create. We're trying to amplify that."
— James Cuda, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "Design is really about solving problems. It's about interfacing with machines in a very human way."
— James Cuda, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "Making something simple is really complex. The beauty of the product is its accessibility."
— James Cuda, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "We're sort of more like musicians than designers. We go into a room, huddle around, and hammer things out."
— James Cuda, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "The customer doesn't see a wireframe; they see a product, and it's much more meaningful when they can interact with that product."
— James Cuda, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "It's very intuitive, but it's not conventional."
— James Cuda, on the Quick Shape feature, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "Iconography is a universal language."
— James Cuda, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "I really believe that half the magic about what we do comes from not being in the valley."
— James Cuda, The Tasmanian Tuxedo profile

> "This is my soul and I'm not prepared to sell it."
— James Cuda, on keeping Savage Interactive independent, The Tasmanian Tuxedo profile

> "iPad Pro was a huge boost to sales. It was a game changer for the creative industry."
— James Cuda, Stockhead interview

> "We want to help amplify our community's natural talent and determination through the tools we make."
— James Cuda, The Design Est interview, 2023

> "Our process is not too dissimilar to making music. We get together and design and develop software until it feels beautiful."
— James Cuda, The Design Est interview

> "It's incredibly humbling to even be nominated for one Apple Design Award, let alone winning for the third time. It is a testament to the team's dedication to making creative tools that empower human creativity."
— James Cuda, on Procreate Dreams winning the 2024 Apple Design Award for Innovation, procreate.com/insight, June 2024

> "Procreate Dreams means a lot to me because it's not just about making movies and animations. Dreams is the perfect word to encapsulate the idea that this app can do anything. It doesn't need to observe the rules of physics, it doesn't have to obey the rules of real life. It can be a dream."
— James Cuda, Procreate Dreams reveal, procreate.com/insight, September 2023

> "I believe my people are the most important thing in my business. If they're happy, they will naturally take care of our customers."
— James Cuda, The Design Est interview

> "Finding Lloyd was like a weight off my shoulders."
— James Cuda, on co-founder Lloyd Bottomley, early company profile

## Claire d'Este (Chief Product Officer)

> "As someone who works at Procreate, for me probably the best thing is we're independent. James is not making decisions about what we're going to do next based on tracking people's data and seeing when they click here or click there. We're doing it much more from the heart."
— Claire d'Este, Creative Bloq interview on Procreate Dreams

> "We've always tried to keep the barrier to entry low and have a friendly first experience. You can jump in and start drawing and it doesn't feel at all intimidating."
— Claire d'Este, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "We always want to add more functionality, but we don't want the app to become overburdened."
— Claire d'Este, Apple Developer: Behind the Design — Procreate

> "A goal we had in developing Procreate Dreams is to bring animation to everyone."
— Claire d'Este, Creative Bloq interview

> "Traditionally animation software has been expensive and complex. We've developed a product that doesn't get in your way, so that every artist can focus on the creative process."
— Claire d'Este, Creative Bloq interview

> "We have been developing Procreate Dreams for over five years."
— Claire d'Este, Creative Bloq interview, 2023
