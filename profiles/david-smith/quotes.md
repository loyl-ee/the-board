# David Smith — Quotes

These quotes are sourced from Smith's publicly accessible writing, podcasts, and documented interviews. Sources are cited inline. Quotes that could not be verified against primary sources are not included.

---

> "Five years ago, a TikTok video changed my life. And I can say that without any hyperbole."

— david-smith.org, "Widgetsmith Five Years Later," September 2025

---

> "Widgetsmith just went viral on TikTok...which is a phrase I don't even really understand but is having a rather robust result on my life right now."

— @_DavidSmith on Twitter/X, September 18, 2020

---

> "A solo, indie developer to build something on their laptop. Hit submit, and then have it end up on over a hundred million people's phones."

— david-smith.org, "Widgetsmith Five Years Later," September 2025

---

> "It took me a long time to get here...there are no guarantees that anything we make will ever find its audience."

— david-smith.org, "Widgetsmith += 100,000,000," March 2023

---

> "I love making apps. It really is that simple."

— david-smith.org, "Let's Get Started," June 2025

---

> "It isn't about being alone, it's about the freedom to choose your own path and then walk it in the manner aligned to your own values."

— david-smith.org, "Independent as in Freedom, not Independent as in Alone," January 2024

---

> "The less I fight back with anachronistic ideas about how software should be sold, the more sustainable a business I have."

— david-smith.org, "Evolving App Store Business Models," September 2016

---

> "Do not start at zero and work up to the price; start at the value you think it is worth and work down (if necessary)."

— david-smith.org, "Negotiating for Nerds," February 2013

---

> "I would rather face difficult problems and climb the mountain of solving them, than cruise along on level ground."

— david-smith.org, "A Developer's View of Vision Pro," June 2023

---

> "The results from a good program, followed consistently, will always outpace the result of a great program followed intermittently."

— david-smith.org, "Choosing Fitness," May 2020

---

> "People are what really matter."

— david-smith.org, "Why Indie Developers Go Insane," February 2014

---

> "Your data is your own. We make no claims to it and will only use it according to the policies outlined below."

— david-smith.org, Pedometer++ Privacy Policy (standing document)

---

> "Only 0.5% of Pedometer++'s active users email support. Only optimize for that vocal minority at the risk of the 99.5% using the app silently."

— david-smith.org / Under the Radar, "Scaling Support," Episode 264, approximate 2023
