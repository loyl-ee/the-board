# Kevin Rose — Quotes

A collection of verifiable quotes from Kevin Rose across interviews, published writing, and public appearances. Only quotes that can be traced to a named source are included.

---

> "I was really good at taking a product and figuring out why it's not working, and how it could be better."

— Startup Grind event interview, circa 2012–2013

---

> "Bootstrapping is the best way. I hate to go out and raise money unless you can prove out an idea first."

— Startup Grind event interview, circa 2012–2013

---

> "If you look yourself in the mirror, every single day, and say I hate this...then you need to change something."

— Startup Grind event interview, on leaving Digg after five years

---

> "I like ideas that aren't me-too ideas. That isn't just an improvement on something."

— Startup Grind event interview, on angel investing criteria

---

> "It all comes down to the way I was feeling a few years ago when Digg kinda blew up. I had a lot of anxiety. I just felt as though I'd been running so hard in the startup environment for so long that my brain needed a reset."

— TechCrunch, on the motivation for building Oak, October 2017

---

> "There are great meditation apps out there that charge for their content and I just don't want to go down that same path. I'm committed to spending the money myself."

— TechCrunch, on Oak's free model, October 2017

---

> "I knew this was a fun body hack to lose weight, but that doesn't mean it's healthy for you. To assess the health benefits (both positive and negative), we need to turn to the only data we can truly trust: research published by scientists in peer-reviewed journals."

— Medium, introducing Zero, 2017

---

> "All of this data is stored on your device and is available for you to export at any time for further analysis."

— Medium, on Zero's data privacy design, 2017

---

> "Our intention with Oak is to teach our users the fundamentals needed to practice app-free traditional meditation."

— Medium, introducing Oak, October 2017

---

> "Zero now is adding 25,000 new users a day at zero paid acquisition. Millions of people use it a month."

— TechCrunch interview, May 2020

---

> "Admitting that you don't know something is just actually growing."

— TechCrunch interview, on lessons from his career, May 2020

---

> "There's also no shame in shutting something down...it's okay to cut bait sometimes."

— TechCrunch interview, May 2020

---

> "When everything is just 'up and to the right' and crazy and chaotic...it's easy to lose your true north."

— Decrypt, on building PROOF during the NFT boom, 2023

---

> "Building in public is really difficult. And so if you show all of your dirty laundry, your community has to be willing to understand that it's messy."

— Decrypt, on the challenges of PROOF's public building process, 2023

---

> "Value has to be seen over years. It can't just happen in three months."

— Decrypt, on NFT market expectations, 2023

---

> "Get it out there!"

— Kevin Rose on Community (Boagworld interview), on shipping vs. deliberating

---

> "The stuff that is creative, the stuff that is unique, the stuff that is hard to do and produce at scale...will stand out."

— Tim Ferriss Show / The Random Show, Episode 391, October 2019

---

> "Rather than do 20 things at once...picking two or three and then really following through on them."

— Tim Ferriss Show / The Random Show, Episode 391, October 2019

---

**Note on quote sourcing:** Several well-circulated attributions to Kevin Rose — including the quote "We tried to do too much at once. We thought we knew better than our community, and we paid the price" regarding the Digg v4 redesign — appear in secondary analyses but could not be traced to a primary source (interview transcript, video, or published article) during research.
