# Dario Amodei — Quotes

Verbatim quotes from documented sources. Where a quote is from a long-form essay, the essay title and year are cited. Where from an interview, the interview and date are cited. All quotes are sourced from publicly available transcripts or published essays.

---

> "One of my main reasons for focusing on risks is that they're the only thing standing between us and what I see as a fundamentally positive future."

— "Machines of Loving Grace," darioamodei.com, October 2024

---

> "I think that most people are underestimating just how radical the upside of AI could be."

— "Machines of Loving Grace," darioamodei.com, October 2024

---

> "We are entering a rite of passage... that will test who we are as a species."

— "The Adolescence of Technology," darioamodei.com, 2025

---

> "Powerful AI will shape humanity's destiny, and we deserve to understand our own creations before they radically transform our economy, our lives, and our future."

— "The Urgency of Interpretability," darioamodei.com, 2025

---

> "If an AI system exhibits certain dangerous capabilities, then we will not deploy it or train more powerful models."

— Prepared remarks, UK AI Safety Summit, anthropic.com, November 2023

---

> "RSPs are not intended as a substitute for regulation, but rather a prototype for it."

— Prepared remarks, UK AI Safety Summit, anthropic.com, November 2023

---

> "It's not about being the good guy, it's about setting things up so that all of us can be the good guy."

— Lex Fridman Podcast #452, November 2024

---

> "When the AI models can do AI research, they become truly, truly autonomous."

— Lex Fridman Podcast #452, November 2024

---

> "The impact of AI might be comparable to that of the industrial and scientific revolutions, but we aren't confident it will go well."

— "Core Views on AI Safety," anthropic.com, 2023

---

> "No one knows how to train very powerful AI systems to be robustly helpful, honest, and harmless."

— "Core Views on AI Safety," anthropic.com, 2023

---

> "As a private actor leading a company developing these AI models, I'm uncomfortable with the significant power this entails — potentially surpassing that of social media companies... The idea of running an AI company with such power is disturbing... the concentration of such power in private hands is fundamentally undemocratic."

— The Ezra Klein Show, New York Times, April 2024

---

> "It is actually very difficult to predict when AI will acquire specific skills or knowledge."

— Prepared remarks, UK AI Safety Summit, anthropic.com, November 2023

---

> "When a generative AI system does something... we have no idea... why it makes the choices it does."

— "The Urgency of Interpretability," darioamodei.com, 2025

---

> "Planning is indispensable, but plans are useless."

— "Core Views on AI Safety," anthropic.com, 2023

---

> "I am optimistic about meaning. I worry about economics and the concentration of power."

— Lex Fridman Podcast #452, November 2024

---

**Note on sourcing:** Amodei gives long, densely argued interviews. The Lex Fridman Podcast #452 transcript (available at lexfridman.com/dario-amodei-transcript/) is one of the most complete records of his views on record and runs to several hours. "Machines of Loving Grace" and "The Adolescence of Technology" at darioamodei.com are the essential primary texts. Any future work with this profile should cross-reference those sources directly.
